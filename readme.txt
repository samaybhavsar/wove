== Description ==

Wove is a minimal, reading-first block theme for a personal website and blog. It pairs a self-hosted serif (Newsreader) with a quiet monochrome palette and automatic dark mode, and keeps the focus on your writing.

Set it up in one click, then make it yours from the backend — no code required:

* A static front page with a personal intro, a year-led "Writing" index, and pages for About and Contact.
* Your email and social links are managed in **Appearance → Wove**, and appear in the footer, on the Contact page, and in the site's structured data.
* Edit your name, bio, and content in the block editor.
* Design (colors, type, spacing) is adjustable in **Appearance → Editor → Styles**.

Features:

* Full Site Editing block theme (theme.json v3).
* Automatic light/dark mode with a header toggle (respects the visitor's OS setting).
* Self-hosted Newsreader font — no external requests.
* Reading time, post categories, polished long-form post styling (lists, tables, images, code).
* Lightweight built-in SEO: meta description, Open Graph, Twitter cards, and JSON-LD.
* Accessibility-minded: skip link, visible focus styles, sensible heading order, reduced-motion support.
* Translation-ready.

== Installation ==

1. In your WordPress admin, go to **Appearance → Themes → Add New → Upload Theme**.
2. Choose the Wove .zip file and click **Install Now**, then **Activate**.
3. When the "Welcome to Wove" notice appears, click **Set up Wove** — this creates your Home, Blog, About and Contact pages, sets the static front page, and enables pretty permalinks.
4. Go to **Appearance → Wove** to add your email and social links.
5. Edit your name and bio on the Home page (or the Intro hero pattern) in the editor.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I change my name, bio, and photo? =
Edit the Home page in the block editor. Replace the placeholder name and bio text, and swap the portrait image for your own.

= Where do I set my social links? =
**Appearance → Wove**. Anything you fill in shows in the footer and on the Contact page, and feeds the site's structured data. Leave a field blank to hide it.

= How do I turn off dark mode? =
Dark mode follows each visitor's operating-system setting, and the header toggle lets them override it. There's no separate setting to disable it.

= Can I change the colors and fonts? =
Yes — **Appearance → Editor → Styles**.
